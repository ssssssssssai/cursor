Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Overview

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we keep it secure, and your choices. By using this site, you agree to this policy.

What we collect

  • Enrollment data: name, email, WhatsApp number, university, college, year, selected subjects or bundle, batch date, payment mode and reference/UTR.
  • Contact messages: name, email, message content.
  • Technical data: device/user agent, page URL, and basic event timestamps.

How we use data

  • Service delivery: enroll you, schedule classes, provide notes and access to learning resources.
  • Payment verification: reconcile UTR/reference to confirm your seat.
  • Support: respond to queries at studental.queries@gmail.com.
  • Safety and compliance: prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, and maintain records.

Legal basis

  • Contract: to provide the course and related services.
  • Legitimate interests: improve services, safeguard our platform, and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., optional communications). You may withdraw consent anytime.

Sharing

  • Vendors: We use Google Apps Script and Google Sheets to store submissions. Data is processed and stored on Google infrastructure.
  • Team: Our tutors and administrators access data to deliver the service.
  • Legal: We may disclose data to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect rights.
  • No selling: We do not sell your personal data.

Security

  • Access control: Google Sheets access is limited to authorized team members.
  • Transport: Data is sent over HTTPS to our Apps Script endpoint.
  • Best efforts: No method is 100% secure; we strive to protect your data using reasonable safeguards.

Data retention

  • Enrollment: retained for up to 5 years for academic and accounting records.
  • Contact messages: retained for up to 24 months.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion by emailing studental.queries@gmail.com from your registered email. We will honor within 30 days unless retention is required by law.

Your choices

  • Access/Update: Email us to access or correct your information.
  • Opt-out: You may opt-out of non-essential communications at any time.

Children

Our services are intended for adult students in professional programs. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child provided data, contact us for deletion.

International transfers

Data may be processed in locations where Google operates. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post changes here with an updated effective date.
